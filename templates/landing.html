Frequently Asked Questions

How do I set up a competition?

Simply log in to your account, click "Create Competition," and follow our step-by-step wizard. You'll have your competition ready in minutes!

Can judges use their phones to score?

Yes! Our platform is fully mobile-responsive, allowing judges to score competitions from any device with a web browser.

Is there a free trial available?

Absolutely! We offer a 14-day free trial on all our plans, no credit card required.

Can I export the results?
